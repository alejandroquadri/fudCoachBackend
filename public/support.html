Subscriptions and billing

All purchases are handled by Apple. You can manage or cancel your subscription from your iPhone: Settings › Apple ID › Subscriptions. If you need help understanding your plan or entitlement in FudCoach, email us at support@fud.coach.

Troubleshooting

  • App not responding or crashing: force close the app and reopen. If issues persist, reboot your device.
  • Entitlement not updating after purchase: open FudCoach and stay on the paywall or profile screen for a few seconds to refresh. If the problem remains, contact us with your Apple receipt or transaction ID.
  • Can’t log food or weight: ensure you’re on the latest app version from the App Store.

Your data requests

To request access to, export of, or deletion of your data, email support@fud.coach from the address linked to your account. We will verify your request and respond as required by applicable law.
